  • "Osmosis"

    Osmosis is a process where water molecules move from an area of low solute concentration to high solute concentration through a semipermeable membrane

  • "Diffusion"

    Diffusion is a natural and physical process, which happens on its own, without stirring or shaking the solutions.

  • "Facilitated Diffusion"

    Facilitated diffusion is the transport of substances across a biological membrane from an area of higher concentration to an area of lower concentration with the help of a transport molecule.
